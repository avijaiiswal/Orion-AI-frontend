Privacy Policy

Data we collect. We store your email address, account status (free/paid/admin), remaining trial count, and any feedback you submit voluntarily through the app. We do not store the text or images you submit to AI tools beyond what is necessary to generate a response.

Authentication. Login is handled via a secure passwordless "magic link" sent to your email through Supabase Auth. We never see or store a password.

Third parties. Generation requests are routed to third-party AI providers (Google Gemini, Groq) solely to produce your requested output. These providers may process your input text/images according to their own policies.

Payments. Payments are handled externally via PayPal; we do not collect or store payment card details.

Your rights. You can request deletion of your account data at any time by contacting support.

Contact. Use the Support link in the app for any privacy-related requests.
