// origin_story

It started late at night. Avi was frustrated — he and Ishan were building their first website, Aao Samjho AI, and kept running into the same problem: every good AI tool was either too expensive, too complicated, or simply not built for Indian creators and students.

The idea hit him at that moment. Not a tool for big corporates. A premium AI hub built specifically for the people around them — startup founders writing their first pitch, creators grinding for viral hooks, small businesses trying to sell better online.

He called Ishan. Ishan didn't just agree — he brought more problems to solve. Together, they mapped out every pain point. Avi started coding. Ishan started planning the business. And Orion Hub was born.

Two students. One city nobody's heard of. Zero budget. Pure execution.
